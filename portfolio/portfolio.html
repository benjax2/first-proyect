WHO AM I?

Hi! I'm Benjamín, a 22 year-old front-end web developer from Montevideo, Uruguay. I'm passionate about crafting intuitive and visually engaging user experiences. My expertise lies in HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, and JavaScript. I also have a solid foundation in back-end development, with experience using PHP and MySQL (skills I developed during my training at FUNVAL).

My curiosity extends beyond coding. In my free time, I dive into the world of music production using tools like Ableton Live and FL Studio. I'm equally passionate about video editing and graphic design, working with Adobe Premiere Pro and Photoshop. I also enjoy immersing myself in video games and getting lost in a good book.
